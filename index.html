Milkshake MADNESS!!

HOW TO PLAY:


  1. A random number is given at the start of the game,
    in the "Match This Number" box.
  2. There are four different types of fruit below:
    • Strawberries
    • Blueberries
    • Bananas
    • Rasberries
  3. Click on the fruit below to add points to your score.
  4. If your score matches the random number, you WIN! If you go over it, you lose.
